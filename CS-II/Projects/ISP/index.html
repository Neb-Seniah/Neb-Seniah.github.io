My ISP is the Mood & Grasp Graph, which is pretty much a way for teachers to gain information from students. The Mood & Grasp Graph uses a machine interface for students to state their current mood and comprehension of the subject being taught. The Data then is displayed on an overhead monitor in the form of a graph. The point of the project is to give the teacher information to work on in class. For example, if the class doesn't get an assignment, then the teacher would be able to see this and review it in class.
